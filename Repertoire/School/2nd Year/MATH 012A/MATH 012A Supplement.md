## Newton-Raphson Method
### Find the Root
**Query**
$f(x) = 3x^3 + 2x^2 + x - 1$

**Find the derivative**
$f’(x) = 3x^2 + 4x + 1$

**Find Next Iteration**
$x_n = x_{n - 1} - \frac{f(x_{n - 1})}{f’(x_{n - 1})}$

**Target**
$|f(x_n)| ≤ 0.0001$

**Solution**

| $x$    | $0$  | $1$ |
| ------ | ---- | --- |
| $f(x)$ | $-1$ | $3$    |

**Table**
$n$, $x_n - 1$, $f(x_n - 1)$, $f’(x_n - 1)$, $x_n$
